ABOUT ME

I am a husband, father & "Fullstack-UI/UX Developer".

As an ESnext junkie I rather develop in pure vanilla what others use jQuery for. And if I bring React.js to a project, a piece of art is made in code.

At age 12 I went to an afternoon art institute to learn composition and color theory. At age 14 I won a junior art contest in my high school. At age 16 I was taught how to draw comics (Marvel/DC style) by a professional comic illustrator. And at age 18 I went to University to study organizational psychology. It may seem odd, but I saw aesthetics and design as my hobby not my career. However, when I moved to Canada and my university studies could not be transferred over, I discovered the digital world (Adobe creative suite) and through word of mouth I started creating designs for business besides my 9-5 job. until one day I was asked to make a website for a cleaning company, I took the challenge and in 1 month I discovered what I really wanted to do as my career...

I am outgoing, creative, and friendly. I am always in pursuit of mentors who will keep me sharp and share new knowledge. I like to meet new people, make friends and help others to become a better version of themselves.
