INSTRUCTIONS

1) "Add" all numbers one by one.
2) When done press "Calculate".
3) Select a calculation.
4) "View Numbers" (optional).
5) "Reset" to start again.


CALCULATIONS

TOTAL = Sum of all numbers.
COUNT = Amount of numbers added.
RANGE = Diference between lowest and highest number.
MEAN = Total divided by Count (average).
MEDIAN = The exact middle number or the Mean of both middle numbers.
MODE = The number that is repeated the most.
